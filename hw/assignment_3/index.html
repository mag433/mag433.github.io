One cold, rainy night, members of the soul- pop trio, SAYGE, had a dream. The lights were dim, they were dressed in black and next to their chairs sat three glasses of cabernet. Now, imagine this dream in the form of beautifully crafted harmonies, melodic lines and lyrics that strike the soul. You have now been formally introduced to SAYGE and their music.
