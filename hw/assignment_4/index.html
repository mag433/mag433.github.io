How To Cook An Egg Over-Easy Style

You will need:

  • frying pan
  • cooking spray
  • 1 egg
  • spatula
  • plate
  • salt and pepper for extra flavor

    1. Step 1: Preparation

      First, you will need to coat your pan with cooking spray. This is to make sure the egg does not stick to the pan. Place your pan on a stovetop, and set the stove to medium heat. You will need to wait one minute for the pan to heat up before you proceed to the next step.

    2. Step 2: Cooking the Egg

      Next, crack the egg onto the pan. Be careful not to break the yolk of the egg. When the whites of the egg begin to cook through and turn white (approx. 90 seconds), proceed to the next step.

    3. Step 3: Flipping the Egg

      Now, you will need to flip the egg, using the spatula. Once you have completed this step, wait until the white is fully cooked through, and then place the egg on a plate. You may add a pinch of salt and pepper for extra taste. Your egg is ready to eat!
