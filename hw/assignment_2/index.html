About Me

Matt Gagnon, from Boston, Massachusetts, has spent most of his life in the classical music world, where he has won state-wide and national awards for his singing, as well as for playing piano and cello. He made the switch to the contemporary world upon beginning his college career at the University of Miami, where he currently studies Music Business and Contemporary Songwriting. He has now released several original songs on SoundCloud, and was featured as a soloist on BisCaydence’s latest a cappella album, titled Against the Current. He hopes to be releasing his first EP as a solo artist by December. After graduation, he plans to work in the music industry as a performer, educator, manager, or producer.
